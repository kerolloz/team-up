What is the idea of TEAM UP?

The main idea is to help students to team up for their graduation project. You know, since our first year, we have always been struggling to team up for a project.

In short, students who haven't joined a team will register. Students who have an idea will search for other students who haven't joined a team yet.

How it works?

  1. If you haven't joined a team yet:
    1. Register.
    2. Verify your email. A verification mail is sent once you register.
    3. Someone will contact you to join his/her team.
    4. Remove yourself. You will find a link in the verification mail.
  2. If you have an idea and searching for team:
    1. Search for a student whose skills match your project needs.
    2. Send him/her an email to see if he would like to join your team.

Why should I register in TEAM UP?

As mentioned before it would make our life easier.

Save the hassle for the project

For example, Let's say that I have an idea for a project. I need a team. My idea will need someone who is good at python. With TEAM UP, that's a pice of cake. I will just search for students who have python in their skill set. Send him/her an email.

On the other hand, If I don't have an idea for a project. I would go register myself, fill up a 3-field small form, and booom. Now, I am into the database, someone will find me suitable to join his project according to my skills.

Easy, isn't it?

Why there is no full list of all the students?

I see no need for this.
You should only see the search results according to skills or name.

Why email, why not facebook profile or phone number?

I see that email would be much easier means of communication, more formal as well. It's up to you, you can ask for the student's facebook profile or phone number when you contact him/her via email.
